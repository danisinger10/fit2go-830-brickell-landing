Your Exclusive Fitness & Wellness Services

Discover a comprehensive suite of services designed to meet all your health and performance needs, right here at 830 Brickell.

Private Personal Training

Achieve your specific fitness goals with one-on-one sessions tailored to your needs, schedule, and abilities. Our expert trainers provide personalized workout plans, motivation, and guidance.

Small Group Fitness Training

Experience the energy and camaraderie of small group training. These sessions offer a motivating environment to build strength, endurance, and functional fitness with personalized attention.

Group Fitness Classes

Choose from a diverse schedule of dynamic group classes, including HIIT, Strength & Conditioning, Core Fusion, and more. Designed to be challenging yet accessible for all fitness levels.

Mobility & Recovery

Enhance your flexibility, reduce muscle soreness, and prevent injuries with dedicated mobility workshops and assisted stretching services. Essential for recovery and long-term performance.

Mindful Yoga Sessions

Cultivate balance, reduce stress, and improve mental clarity with our range of yoga classes, from energizing Vinyasa flows to restorative Yin practices, suitable for all levels.

Wellness Workshops & Nutrition

Engage in informative workshops on topics like stress management, nutrition fundamentals, and ergonomic assessments to support your holistic well-being and healthy habits.
